Shirowot:Ethiopians traditional food

What is Shiro wot?

Shiro Wot is The Ultimate Ethiopian Comfort Feed. It is Hearty, Delicious Belt Very Easy to Prepare.

Ingredients to make Shiro:

  • 1 Onion Red or Regular
  • 2 Tomatoes Diced
  • 3 Cups of Water
  • 3-4 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • ½ Cups of Mitten Shiro Flour Buy it
  • 2-7 Cloves Of Garlic
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • Black Pepper as Heeded
  • Fassica’s Injera Bread

Steps to make Shiro Wot:

  1. In A Large Stock Pot, Cook The Onion on Medium-high Heat for Several Minutes, Stirring Frequently, Until They Begin To soften and Then Translucent.

  2. And Now Add the Oil and Cook For A Few More Minutes. If Berbere is Added, Allow the Ingredients to Simmer Tighter For Several Minutes.

  3. Continue to Saute For Another 2-3 Minutes And Then Add Tomatoes

  4. Cook For 4-5 Minutes. Add ½ of Water and Continue to Simmer.

  5. Add Salt

  6. Start Adding a Small Portion of Mitten Shiro Flour at A Time and While Continuously.

  7. Shiro Will Start to Becoming Thicker.

  8. Let it Cook For 15-20 Minutes.

  9. Continuously Adding a Small Amount of Water, While Stirring for 1-2 Minutes or Until Desire Shiro Thickness.

  10. Shiro Stew Will Continue to Become Thicker With More Cooking time. It’s Always Best To Add A Small Amount of Extra Water and Let it Cook For Additional 4-5 Minutes.

  11. Add The Garlic and Salt, Stir Well, And Continue Simmering Until Bubbles Start appearing For 2-3 Minutes.

  12. Serve
